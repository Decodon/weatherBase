WeatherTop is an web application designed for the amateur meteorologist.

A simple design, it allows you to enter in details such as:

  • Weather Codes
  • Temperature
  • Wind Speed
  • Pressure
  • Wind Direction



It then can perform calculations on the inputs and display the latest details in a quick and easy to read format


It also allows you to create new stations and locations for the meteorologist on the go.
You know, if you want to chase tornados around Oklahoma after you saw Bill Paxton doing it
